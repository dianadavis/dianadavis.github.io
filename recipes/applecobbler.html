Apple Cobbler
From Leona Bischoff



Mix together and put in the bottom of a 9 x 9 pan:
5 c. sliced peeled apples
1/2 c. sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon

Topping:
1 c. sugar
3/4 c. flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
4 Tbsp melted butter

Mix together and place topping over the apples. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes (longer for fall apples). Can double and bake in 9 x 13 pan.
