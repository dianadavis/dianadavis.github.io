Susie's Donuts
From Susan Poole Davis (Daddow)



2 eggs, beaten
1 c. sugar
5 tsp butter
1 c. sour milk
4 c. flour
1/2 tsp ginger
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp baking soda
2 tsp baking powder

Mix in order given. Roll and handle as little as possible. Fry in hot deep fat. Yield: 5 dozen.
