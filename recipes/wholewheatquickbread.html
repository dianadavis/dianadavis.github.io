Whole Wheat Quick Bread


2 cups whole wheat flour

1 tsp. baking soda

2 tsp. baking powder

1 tsp. salt


Sift the above into a bowl.


Then add:


1/2 cup soy flour

 6 T. corn oil

1 1/2 cups sour milk 

1/2 cup molasses (or honey) 

1/4 cup dry milk


Stir well.  Spoon into buttered 9 x 5 loaf pan.


Let stand 20 minutes.  


Heat oven to 350.


Bake about 60 minutes or until bread is nicely browned and tests done with a toothpick.


A lovely bread, especially for breakfast. 


I usually add at least 1/2 cup raisins.


I made this the first time I came to Carol’s house for dinner, a week after we met.  She was really impressed that I not only could cook, but that I had made a loaf of bread.   We’ve been enjoying it for 25 years!
