Blueberry Muffins



Mix well:
1 egg
1/2 c. milk
1/4 c. vegetable oil

Mix together separately:
1 1/2 c. flour
1/2 c. sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture and add:
1 c. blueberries

Mix until just blended. Pour into greased muffin tin. You can sprinkle them with cinnoman and sugar before baking. Bake at 400 for 20-25 minutes. Yield: 1 dozen.
