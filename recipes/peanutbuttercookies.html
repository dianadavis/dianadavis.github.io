Peanut Butter Cookies



Cream:
1 stick butter
1 c. brown sugar

Add to creamed mixture:
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 c. smooth peanut butter

Combine, then stir into above:
1 1/2 c. flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt

Chill dough. Then make 1-inch round balls and place 2 inches apart on baking sheet. Press slightly with fork in both directions. Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes.
