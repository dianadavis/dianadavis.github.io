Banana Bread



Combine:
1 3/4 c. flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt

Cream:
1/3 c. shortening
2/3 c. sugar
2 eggs

Mash 1 c. bananas

Add half of the bananas to the creamed mixture, then add the flour mixture, then add the rest of the bananas. Bake at 350 for 1 hour or less.
