Unbaked Orange Balls
From Leona Bischoff



2 1/2 c. crushed graham crackers - or vanilla wafers, or add part lightly salted crushed pretzels
1 stick margarine, softened
1 c. flaked coconut
2 c. powdered sugar (measure, then sift)
6 oz. slightly thawed frozen orange juice concentrate

Combine. Roll into balls, size of walnuts. Roll each ball in finely chopped walnuts. Chill until set. Do not need refrigeration.
