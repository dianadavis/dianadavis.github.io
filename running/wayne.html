Retired engineering executive Wayne Peacock will share his story and unique perspectives on life and running. Wayne will tell us that while we were born with the capacity to run into our 70s, 80s and 90s, regrettably few from his generation are able to do so. Wayne looks at this diminished quality of life for our elders with sorrow yet knowing that future elders are fully capable of preventing the vast majority of afflictions and suffering. The answer lies in new social norms that call forth personal responsibility for ones health and fitness.
